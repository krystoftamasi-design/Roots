About
The ROOTS project brings together five creative studios from Tomas Bata University in Zlín with a single goal: to transform Czech and Slavic traditions into modern product design. This interdisciplinary collaboration results in a unique fusion of cultural heritage and the contemporary visual language of the younger generation. Spanning a collection of original porcelain, jewelry, and footwear, custom-designed packaging, and a complex architectural exhibition layout, the ROOTS project demonstrates how traditional identity can be preserved, redefined, and passed on in modern society.
